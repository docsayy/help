Apical 4-Chamber (A4C)
How to Get It
  • Probe at apex (PMI)
  • Indicator ‚Üí left side
  • Angle upward toward base of heart
What You See
  • LV, RV, LA, RA
  • Mitral & Tricuspid valves
What A4C Helps With

  • RV size/function
  • Chamber enlargement
  • Pericardial effusion check
RV Strain Clues
  • RV > LV in size
  • Septal flattening (also see PSAX)
  • Reduced RV free-wall motion
