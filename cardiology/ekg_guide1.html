3Rhythm: regular vs irregular (and why)

Rate rule: Irregular + fast can be the whole story (AF with RVR) in dyspnea/hypotension.
Atria rule: Identify atrial activity: organized P-waves vs fibrillatory/Flutter waves = chronic clues + acute management.
  • Regular: sinus tachy, atrial flutter with fixed block, SVT, VT.
  • Irregularly irregular: AF (common), multifocal atrial tachycardia (MAT), frequent ectopy.
